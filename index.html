I will be joining a Master's program in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Michigan starting this Fall 2021. Before, I was an undergrad here and had worked on several fun research projects on deep reinforcement learning under Honglak Lee and Satinder Singh.

My research interest is in the intersection of deep learning and reinforcement learning (RL) and its various real-world applications. Specifically, I am interested in building efficient and task-agnostic intelligent machine with hierarchical RL and meta-learning.
