I studied Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Michigan, where I am currently working on a research projects in deep reinforcement learning as a research assistant under Honglak Lee. Previously, I was advised by Satinder Singh and Gunhee Kim.

My research interest is in the intersection of deep learning and reinforcement learning (RL) and its various real-world applications such as robotics. Specifically, I am interested in building efficient and task-agnostic intelligent machine with hierarchical RL and meta-learning.
